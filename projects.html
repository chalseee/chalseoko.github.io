Projects I have worked on in spare time.

kapability.

Kapability is an application that connects employers with job-seekers who have a disability, improving overall client visibility, job opportunities, and financial mobility. Offering unique services caered towards clients with disabilities, Kapability allows them to find work quicker and more efficiently.

the awesomizer.

The Awesomizer is a website that is focused on increasing engagmenet and interaction between the library and those who use it. Drawing from the University of Michigan library system, the website allows users to upload library resources that are commonly enjoyed to a website that is shared with the university community.
