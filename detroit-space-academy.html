mission.

Through our academies, we wish to provide STEM exposure to students in the Detroit and metro Detroit area, inspire them to pursue higher education, teach about leadership, quality and excellence through experience that enforce a core STEM curriclum.

    We envision a world where black students:
  • Pursue STEM fields in higher education.
  • Are aware of the many opportunities in aerospace and space fields.
  • Eagerly give back to the city of Detroit.
  • Care about their education
